Project Overview

This Tableau project explores *IPL (Indian Premier League)* data to analyze team and player performance across multiple seasons.
It focuses on identifying the *best teams, **top players, and **key match-winning insights* through interactive dashboards and visual storytelling.

 Objectives

* Analyze IPL data to find most consistent and impactful players.
* Identify top-performing teams based on win rate, total runs, wickets, and player contributions.
* Explore match trends, toss impact, and venue advantages.
* Create a storytelling dashboard to summarize insights visually.

Dashboards & Stories

Dashboard 1 — IPL Case Study

IPL Casestudy_v2025.1.twbx
Highlights:

* Top 10 players by runs, wickets, and strike rate.
* Team comparison by win percentage, average score, and match outcomes.
* Venue-based performance map.
* Interactive filters for teams, seasons, and venues.
* Toss analysis: “Does winning the toss help win the match?”

Dashboard 2 — IPL Story

Story_Satya Supriya_v2025.1.twbx

Highlights:

* Visual storytelling of IPL’s most successful players and teams.
* Comparison of performance trends across seasons.
* Summary story combining all analytical dashboards into a single flow.
* Final conclusion identifying *Best Team* and *Best Player* based on data-driven metrics.

Key Insights

* Best Team: Mumbai Indians (highest win %, consistent playoff appearances).
* Best Player: Virat Kohli (most runs), MS Dhoni (finisher impact), Jasprit Bumrah (best economy among bowlers).
* Most Successful Venue: Wankhede Stadium shows highest team scores and win consistency.
* Toss advantage is significant in evening matches (batting second often leads to wins).
